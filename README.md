
# Airbnb-dashboard

1 Short Description

The **BEYOND BOOKINGS** is an interactive Power BI project built to explore Airbnb listings across multiple cities. It provides insights into market distribution, pricing, customer satisfaction, and listing growth through interactive visualizations. To enhance the user experience, the dashboard includes **Power BI Bookmarks**, allowing users to switch between **Overall Rating (⭐)** and **Detailed Rating (💚)** views without navigating to another page.

2  Tech Stack

* **Microsoft Power BI Desktop** – Dashboard development
* **Power Query** – Data cleaning and transformation
* **DAX** – Measures and calculated columns
* **Power BI Bookmarks & Selection Pane** – Interactive navigation
* **CSV Dataset** – Data source
  
3 Data Source

* **Dataset:** Airbnb Listings Dataset
* **Source:** Maven Analytics
* **Data Includes:** Listings, Hosts, Cities, Room Types, Property Types, Pricing, Reviews, Availability, and Customer Ratings.

4 Features & Highlights

##  Business Problem

With Airbnb operating across multiple cities and property types, understanding market performance from raw data alone is challenging. Stakeholders need a way to quickly identify where demand is highest, how pricing differs, and which markets deliver the best customer experience.

This dashboard transforms raw listing data into an interactive report that makes these patterns easy to explore and compare.

##  Goal of the Dashboard

The dashboard was designed to:

* Analyze Airbnb's market presence across different cities.
* Compare accommodation pricing across room types.
* Monitor listing growth over time.
* Evaluate customer satisfaction using rating metrics.
* Help users explore insights through an interactive dashboard experience.


5  Dashboard Walkthrough

## Business Snapshot

The KPI cards provide a quick summary of Airbnb's marketplace by displaying:

* Total Listings
* Total Hosts
* Total Cities
* Total Property Types
* Total Reviews

## Listing Growth Analysis

Tracks how Airbnb listings have evolved over time and compares the contribution of different room types to the platform's growth.

## Market Share Analysis

A Pareto chart highlights the cities that contribute the largest share of Airbnb listings, helping identify dominant and emerging markets.

## Pricing Analysis

Compares the average price across room types, making it easy to distinguish between premium and budget accommodation categories.

## Customer Rating Analysis

A unique feature of this dashboard is the use of **Power BI Bookmarks**.

Instead of creating separate report pages:

* **⭐ Yellow Star** displays the **Overall Rating** of each city.
* **💚 Green Star** switches to a **Detailed Rating Heatmap**, showing ratings for Cleanliness, Communication, Check-in, Accuracy, Location, and Value.

This creates a cleaner, more interactive reporting experience.


6 Business Impact & Insights

* Paris dominates the Airbnb market with the highest number of listings.
* Entire Place accommodations represent the largest share of listings, reflecting traveler preferences.
* Hotel Rooms command the highest average prices, indicating a premium pricing segment.
* Listing growth accelerated rapidly before stabilizing, with a noticeable decline during the COVID-19 period.
* Mexico City and Rio de Janeiro stand out for consistently high customer satisfaction scores.
* Bookmark navigation improves report usability by enabling users to switch between summary and detailed rating analysis without changing pages.
